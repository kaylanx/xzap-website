Xzap for iOS

Xzap was originally programmed programmed by Mark Wirt and was released in 1984 for the Commodore 16 (C16).

Software Engineer Andy Kayley had a Commodore 16 when he was 10 years old and Xzap was his favourite game. He has been writing a clone of Xzap for iOS using Swift and SceneKit.
